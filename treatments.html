The Science of Recovery

01

HIV vs. AIDS: The Critical Difference

Being HIV positive does not mean you have AIDS. HIV is the virus; AIDS is the advanced stage that only occurs if the virus is left untreated. Our clinical goal is to keep the virus suppressed so you never reach that stage.

02

The "One Pill" Reality

Modern treatment is no longer a complex struggle. For most, it is a single daily tablet with minimal side effects. As long as the "molecular lock" of your medicine is in place, your immune system remains resilient.

03

U=U: The Guarantee of Life

Undetectable = Untransmittable. Once your treatment suppresses the viral load to undetectable levels, you can live a full, normal life, and the virus cannot be passed to others. This is the scientific victory we aim for with every patient.
