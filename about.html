Dr. J.K. Maniar

Dr. J.K. Maniar is a pioneer in infectious disease medicine in India. With a career spanning over five decades, he is recognized for his calm, honest approach to patient care, handling sensitive conditions with absolute confidentiality and precision.

As the lead architect of the National HIV Guidelines (NACO), his work defines the clinical standards followed by the Ministry of Health. Internationally, he serves as a visiting professor at UTMB Texas, USA, bridging global medical research with ethical local practice.
